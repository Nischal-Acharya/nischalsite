Privacy Policy

Popup Translate – Your Privacy Matters

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Popup Translate collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to transparency and data protection.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects minimal information necessary to provide its translation services:

  • Selected text on webpages for translation.
  • User settings such as preferred target language and auto-translate toggle.

We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, or browsing history.

How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Translating selected text instantly.
  • Saving your preferred language and settings.

No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Third-Party Services

Popup Translate may use third-party translation APIs to perform translations. These APIs may temporarily process selected text to provide translations, but do not store your data permanently.

Storage & Security

All user settings are stored locally in your browser. We implement security best practices to protect your data and ensure that only the extension has access to your settings.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Context Menus: To offer translation options via right-click.
  • Host Permissions: To access selected text on webpages.
  • Storage: To save user preferences.

These permissions are only used for the extension’s core functionality and nothing else.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Users will be informed of major changes through the extension’s update notes.
