For developers or advanced users, you can install QuickCalc manually:

  2. Enable developer mode in your browser
  3. Load the unpacked extension

About QuickCalc

QuickCalc was created to solve a simple but common problem: the need for a quick calculation without interrupting your workflow. Whether you're a student working on homework, a developer debugging code, or a professional working with numbers, QuickCalc provides instant access to a calculator right in your browser.

Our Mission

We believe in creating tools that enhance productivity without complexity. QuickCalc is designed to be simple yet powerful, respecting your privacy while providing a seamless experience across both Firefox and Chrome browsers.

Privacy Focused

Unlike many other extensions, QuickCalc does not collect any personal data. Your calculations remain private and are never sent to any server. We're committed to keeping it that way.
