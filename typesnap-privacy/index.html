TypeSnap Privacy Policy

Introduction

TypeSnap is a browser extension designed to help users expand text snippets quickly and efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how TypeSnap handles your data.

Data Collection

TypeSnap does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All text snippets, preferences, and settings are stored locally in your browser. Audio feedback files included in the extension are also stored locally.

Local Storage

All snippets and settings are saved using the browser's local storage API. Only you can access this data on your device. TypeSnap does not send this data to any server or third-party service.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Used to detect the current tab for snippet expansion.
  • storage: Used to save your snippets, preferences, and selected audio locally.
  • scripting: Used locally to detect triggers and expand snippets in input fields, textareas, and contenteditable elements.

Third-Party Services

TypeSnap does not use any third-party services, analytics, or trackers. Everything runs locally within your browser.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
